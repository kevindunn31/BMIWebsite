What is BMI and Whats Your BMI

"What is BMI? BMI stands for Body Mass Index. It could assist with determing your body type.

It takes in two factors (Height and Weight). Everybody should fall into a range where you are considered a healthy height to weight ratio.

This couls tell an individual if they are under, over, obese, or normal body type. This information is not always perfect and can be skewed in

why you might fall in a certain category on the BMI scale."
